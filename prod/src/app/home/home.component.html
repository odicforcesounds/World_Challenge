Welcome to the World Wide Brands Competition

Join us in our exciting competition where we challenge individuals, businesses, and organizations to make a positive impact on society and the environment through their actions.

Our challenge is designed to be educational and focuses on simple actions that can improve lives and benefit all living beings.
It serves as a tool to guide the choices of children and adults alike, encouraging them to make conscious decisions that contribute to a better world.

We invite all individuals and businesses that represent any kind of logo, including religious groups, sports groups, political groups, social groups, and commercial entities, to participate in this competition.
The goal is to promote waste reduction, sustainable practices, and social responsibility within the business world.

We believe that the best way to learn is through practice, and by making these simple choices, we can inspire and motivate others to make better choices as well.

Challenge Objectives:

  • Educate and motivate world-wide brands to prioritize social and environmental principles within their business operations
  • Foster better relationships in society by promoting care and respect for all living beings
  • Associate economic value with actions that have inherent value, rather than solely focusing on profit-driven choices
  • Promote positive behaviors and actions at all levels of society, giving them the recognition and value they deserve

How the Challenge Works:

Participants will be evaluated based on their positive contributions and actions, with each contribution earning a certain number of coins.
The challenge covers various aspects, including students' behaviors and choices, social behaviors and choices, commercial behaviors and choices, and professional behaviors and choices.

Negative contributions will not be rated or promoted; instead, the focus will be on discussing and presenting solutions to address and rectify them.
It is important to highlight and promote good behaviors rather than allowing negative behaviors to overshadow them.

Rewards and Recognition:

Participants and companies will have the opportunity to earn rewards and recognition for their positive contributions.
This includes public exposition as a group that has made significant contributions, as well as internal recognition among colleagues and clients.

Furthermore, the challenge extends to students and schools, where positive behaviors, knowledge acquisition, and sharing will be rewarded.
By participating, students can earn points, game status, professional status, civic status, real gifts, and valuable life experiences.

Join the Challenge:

If you are passionate about making a difference, we invite you to join the World Wide Brands Competition.
Together, let's build a better world by promoting sustainable practices, social responsibility, and positive actions.
